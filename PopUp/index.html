Hey You

Embrace yourself! You are unique and wonderful just as you are. Let go of the desire to be someone else or to seek approval from those who bring you down. Stop criticizing your body, face, personality, and quirks—embrace them! These qualities define you, and without them, you wouldn't be the amazing person you are. Why wish to be anyone else?
Confidence looks good on you. Smile and let your happiness radiate. If anyone dislikes you for being content with who you are, let them go. Your happiness shouldn't rely on others. Declare proudly, "I am happy because I love myself. I cherish my flaws and imperfections—they make me unique. And this version of 'me' is absolutely awesome!"
